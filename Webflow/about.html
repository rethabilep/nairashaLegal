Our Story

We are a virtual law firm whose sole desire is to ensure that everyone has access to a lawyer. Every time, Anytime, Anywhere.
We use the internet and technology to ensure that everyone has access to a lawyer, thus shortening the time and breaking usual barriers to seeking and finding justice. We want to match and support vulnerable groups with legal skills that not only suit their budget, but also are a perfect fit to their needs.

Our debut program “senakangoeli” which also is our driving force is intended to provide assistance and support to women and children in vulnerable situations beyond normal working hours. Aware that a staggering 86% of Basotho women have been in intimate partner violence of one degree or another, our aim is to respond to these cases as and when they are lodged onto our system. Our premise is that rape and violence do not wait for 9-5 nor do they observe weekends and holidays. There is need for efficient and prompt support for those in need at times when they need it the most.
‍
This could be by way of services coordination, support or sending resources that may be needed to preserve both the life and dignity of the individual involved. By liaising with different service providers, we hope to provide a well-oiled, instant, and cost effective support platform that will not only change lives, but one that will directly affect both our HIV statistics and the number of children left orphaned or traumatized by violence or distress.

Our Mission

  • To ensure that everyone has access to justice as and when they need.
  • To ensure that no oneis rendered vulnerable because of their ignorance of the law or services, and to do so withdignity, respect and minimal discomfort to the individual.

Our Guiding Principles

  • One life is every Life.
  • ‘Ha le fete khomo le je motho’, nothing should be spared where a life is involved.
  • If justice is the pathway to the enjoyment of rights, then everyone is entitled to a lawyer.

Our Services

  • Low bono legal services to vulnerable groups, in particular, women, children and youth.
  • Lawyer client pairing and support for all.
  • Legal services for start-up companies led by women, youth and groups made vulnerable by either economies, violation of rights, age or other classifications as may be.
  • Legal support for communities.
  • Online legal support for victims of women and children in abusive situations.
  • Training and technical legal support for government and other institutions.
